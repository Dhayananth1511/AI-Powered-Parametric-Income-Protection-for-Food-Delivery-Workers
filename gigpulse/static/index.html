4 Steps. Fully Automatic.

From disruption detection to money in your UPI wallet — all within minutes.

Step 01
📡

Step 02
⚡

Trigger Detection

When rainfall exceeds 35mm/3hr, temperature hits 43°C, AQI crosses 300, or a cyclone/curfew alert fires — disruption is flagged.
